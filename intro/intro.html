  1. Welcome to Code Oholics, the ultimate coding club for students at our college! Are you passionate about coding, programming, and all things tech? Do you want to join a community of like-minded individuals who share your love for computer science? Then look no further, because Code Oholics is the club for you.

  2. Mission is to create a welcoming and inclusive space for students who want to learn, grow, and explore the world of coding. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or a beginner just starting out, we welcome all levels of experience and skill.

  3. At Code Oholics, we offer a variety of events and activities, such as coding workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and guest speakers from industry professionals. We also provide opportunities for members to collaborate on coding projects, build their portfolios, and network with other students and professionals in the field.

  4. Joining Code Oholics is a great way to enhance your coding skills, meet new people, and gain experience that will be valuable for your future career. So come join us and become a part of our community of Code Oholics!
